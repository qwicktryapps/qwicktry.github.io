FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is this extension?

AtoZ Shift Helper is a Chrome browser extension that helps Amazon AtoZ users filter the available shift list by their preferred dates, days of the week, and time windows. Instead of manually scrolling through every available shift, you set your criteria and the extension surfaces only the ones that match.

Do I need an Amazon AtoZ account?

Yes. This extension works on the Amazon AtoZ scheduling platform. You need an active Amazon AtoZ account to use it.

What browser does it work on?

Google Chrome only. The extension is available on the Chrome Web Store and requires Chrome version 88 or later.

What is a license key?

Your license key is a unique code you receive by email when you sign up. You enter it in the extension popup along with your Employee ID to activate your access. Each license is tied to one Employee ID.

Can I share my license with someone else?

No. Each license is bound to one Amazon Employee ID on first use. If you try to use it with a different Employee ID, activation will be declined. You can purchase additional licenses for other accounts.

Is my Amazon account information safe?

Yes. The extension does not access, store, or transmit your Amazon account credentials, password, or personal information. It only reads the shift listings displayed on the AtoZ page. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
